Account Management

Delete Your Account

You have the right to delete your Matrix Fist account and all associated data at any time. This page explains how, what gets deleted, and what (if anything) is retained.

01 — In-App Deletion

Delete From the App

The fastest way to delete your account is directly inside the Matrix Fist app. No email required.

  1. Open the Matrix Fist app and sign in to your account
  2. Tap the Profile tab at the bottom of the screen The person icon, bottom right
  3. Scroll to the bottom of your profile page
  4. Tap Delete Account Shown in red at the bottom of the settings section
  5. Confirm deletion when prompted This action is immediate and cannot be undone

Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted immediately. You will be signed out and will not be able to sign in again with the same email unless you create a new account.

02 — Request by Email

Can't Access the App?

If you are unable to access your account or the app, you can request deletion by contacting us directly.

Send a Deletion Request

Email us from the address associated with your Matrix Fist account. Include your @handle if you know it. We will process your request within 7 business days.

Email Deletion Request matrixfist.official@gmail.com
03 — Data Deletion

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently and immediately deleted from our systems:

Data Type Description Status
Account credentials Email address, password hash, authentication tokens Deleted immediately
Profile data Handle, display name, bio, location, age group, country Deleted immediately
Profile photo Avatar image uploaded to your profile Deleted immediately
Match records All wins, losses, and match history involving your account Deleted immediately
Challenges All sent and received challenge records Deleted immediately
Notifications All in-app alerts and notification history Deleted immediately
Push token Device push notification token Deleted immediately
EULA acceptance Record of terms acceptance Deleted immediately
04 — Data Retention

What Is Retained

Matrix Fist does not retain any personal data after account deletion. There is no backup retention period for personal information.

The only exception is anonymised, aggregated statistics (e.g. total number of matches played across all users) that contain no personally identifiable information and cannot be linked back to your account.

Standard server access logs (IP addresses, timestamps) held by our infrastructure provider (Supabase) may be retained for up to 30 days for security purposes before automatic deletion. These are not shared with third parties.

05 — Data We Collect

What Matrix Fist Collects

For full transparency, here is every category of data Matrix Fist collects during normal use:

Category Data Purpose
Account Email address, password (hashed) Authentication only
Public profile Handle, display name, country, city, age group Displayed on leaderboard and trade card
Photo Profile picture (optional) Displayed on profile and trade card
Match data Win/loss records, match timestamps, opponent handles Core app functionality and ranking
Device Push notification token In-app alerts (win confirmations, challenges)
Location City, country (user-entered, not GPS) Profile display only

Matrix Fist does not collect GPS location, contacts, browsing history, financial information, or any data not listed above. Data is never sold to third parties.

All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections and stored on Supabase infrastructure with encryption at rest.
