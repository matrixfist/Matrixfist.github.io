Official Rules

The Player Card

Everything you need to play, score, and compete in Matrix Fist — wherever you are.

🧾

The Vitruvian Standard

Stand side by side, shoulder to shoulder, facing opposite directions. Arms at natural extension. Eyes always forward — never look at your opponent sideways.

✊

Scoring

Grip the forearm, wrist, hand, or fingers. Hold for 1 second = 1 point. Call your score aloud: "1", "2"... "WIN".

First to 5 points wins (players 12 and under).
First to 10 points wins (players 13 and older).

🚫

No Fists — Blocking Rule

Blocking is open palms only. Fists are NEVER permitted in any form. No striking, punching, or closed-fist contact of any kind. Violations result in immediate disqualification.

💨

The Yank

A gripped player may yank once to break free. Masters hold through it.

📋

Match Rules

  • Unused hand behind back — mandatory
  • No pulling — grip only, never drag
  • Switch arms between rounds
  • Shake hands after every round
  • Mutual verbal agreement required before every match
  • Trim fingernails before play
✅

Safety Check

Before every match: trim fingernails, check for injuries, ensure clear space around both players, and confirm mutual agreement to play.

📱

Log Every Win

Download the app. Enter your opponent's handle. They confirm. The record is permanent and public. No confirmation = no win.
