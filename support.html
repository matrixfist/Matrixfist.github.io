Frequently Asked Questions
How does match verification work? +
When you log a win against another player, they receive an alert in the app to confirm or reject the result. A win only counts toward your record once your opponent confirms it. If they reject, the match is flagged as disputed and no record is updated.
My opponent confirmed the win but my record didn't update. +
Try closing and reopening the app — your profile loads fresh on each session. If the issue persists after a restart, email us at matrixfist.official@gmail.com with your username and the approximate time of the match.
How do I reach the next rank? +
Rank is based on your confirmed win count. The thresholds are: Initiate (0+), Contender (10+), Fighter (25+), Warrior (50+), Pro (100+), Elite (200+), Master (400+), Grand Master (2,500+). Wins must be confirmed by your opponent to count. Visit the Practice Guide for rank-specific training tips.
How do I unlock the rank store? +
When you reach a new rank, the app sends you a notification with a link to your exclusive store. Initiate through Warrior stores are open automatically. Pro, Elite, Master, and Grand Master stores are invite-only — email matrixfist.official@gmail.com with your rank and username to request your webcode.
I can't find another player to challenge. +
Players must have an account to appear in search. Use their exact username (handle) — search is case-sensitive. If you're playing someone in person, they can create a free account in under a minute at the Get the App link above.
How do I turn off daily practice notifications? +
Open the app and go to your Profile (bottom right). Scroll down to find the "Daily Practice Reminders" toggle and switch it off. The setting saves automatically and stops all scheduled reminders immediately.
I forgot my password or can't log in. +
On the login screen, tap "Forgot password" to receive a reset link by email. If you no longer have access to your email, contact us at matrixfist.official@gmail.com and we'll help verify your account manually.
How do I delete my account? +
Go to Profile → scroll to the bottom → tap "Delete Account." This permanently removes your profile, win/loss record, and all associated data. This action cannot be undone. Alternatively, email us and we can process the deletion on our end.
Where can I read the full rules? +
The complete rules and player card are available at matrixfist.com/player-card. The app also has a How to Play section in the Play tab with swipeable rule cards.
