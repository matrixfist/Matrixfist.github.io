The Game

How to Play

Side by side. Eyes forward. First to ten wins.

🧾
The Position
Stand side by side, shoulder to shoulder, facing opposite directions. Arms at natural extension. Eyes always forward.
✊
Scoring
Grip the forearm, wrist, hand, or fingers. Hold for 1 second = 1 point. First to 5 (under 12) or 10 (13+).
🚫
No Fists — Ever
Blocking is open palms only. No striking, punching, or closed-fist contact of any kind. Violations = immediate disqualification.
💨
The Yank
A gripped player may yank once to break free. Masters hold through it.
📋
The Rules
Unused hand behind back. No pulling. Switch arms between rounds. Shake hands after every round.
📱
Log Every Win
